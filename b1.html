GITAM (Deemed to be University) adheres to three basic principles: Strive, Serve, Thrive. This sums up the quintessence of human life that only by seeking the purpose of service can one prosper in life. We move forward with the firm determination to excel in all academic matters. Established in 1980, The Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management (GITAM), was founded by Dr. M.V. V. S. Murthi, former MP and philanthropist, and a group of inspired intellectuals and industrialists. At GITAM, we strive to create an environment that values diversity, promotes an inclusive culture, and creates a deep sense of belonging for every member of our community. Vision To become a global leader in higher education. Mission Provide a futuristic and comprehensive education according to global standards with a high level of discipline and social relevance in a calm and stimulating environment.
